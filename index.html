About School

MP PUBLIC SCHOOL (MPPS) have been established to introduce intractive Consciousness based Education in mainstream of school education in India.We have 2 schools in MP state, Rewa Distt with about 700 students and about 50 teaching, administrative and support staff has proven its importance in Indian society for last 8 years. Our Values

The aim of the school is to train students to work intelligently at the subject matter of the standard they are placed in and currently to develop in them the various habits which their future life as upright citizens demands of them. The School provides all round education to the students by promoting various activities.

Mission:

TO CREATE A HIGHER ORDER OF SOCIETY ENRICHING THE INDIVIDUALS WITH KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND VALUES.

Vision:

TO MOTIVATE EACH CHILD TO STRIVE FOR EXCELLENCE IN ORDER TO BECOME A COMPASSIONATE, COGNIZANT AND PRODUCTIVE BEING .
